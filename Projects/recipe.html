Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500mg white unbleached flour
    or
    250mg white unbleached flour, and 250mg semolina flour
  • 5 eggs (preferable room temperature

Directions

  1. Put flour in mixing bowl
  2. Put eggs in mixing bowl in the middle of flour without the shells
  3. Mix together until mostly combined
  4. Take the dough out of the bowl onto a clean counter
  5. Knead until elastic
  6. Roll until about 1/2 cm thick
  7. Cut into strips
  8. Cook for about 2-4 minutes in salted boiling water
  9. Enjoy with favorite toppings!
